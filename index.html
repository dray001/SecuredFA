SecuredFA

This is a system that provides API first 2 factor authentication and enterprise OTP generation and delivery via several channels: SMS, VOICE, EMAIL, WHATSAPP. It removes the complexity of OTP creation and management from your development process and let you focus on other things that are more important to your solution. The solution is enterprise ready, built for seamless integration from ground up.
